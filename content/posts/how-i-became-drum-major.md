# What being a drum major is like


In my band program, being a Drum Major is the closest thing you can be to become a staff member as a student. Drum Majors work closely with staff members and we get to talk about a lot of logistical things about running the band, and how we can effectively lead them to success.

# What being a drum major is not like

I came into high school completely quitting band. Much like many other kids, I was done with band. After covid, and an overall distaste and loss of passion for my instrument, I told myself I would completely quit and "find something else". At that point, I didn't know the extent to how vague that was and how nothing else in high school would be as big as a commitment as band did. If it wasn't for the Drum Majors during my freshmen then, I truly wouldn't know where I would be today. At that point, my director, knew about my feelings about band.
